> Contest Rules

Qualification Round

Each team plays 50 matches, and the 16 teams with the higest ranking are selected for the final round.

Final Round

The Final Round is carried out in a tournament.

The 4 selected teams in the 16 teams play a single game against each other, and the winner advances to the final match. The final ranking is determined by the score of the final match.

Exibition Round

Four teams, composed of the best 2 teams of the final round and the best 2 teams of the sponsors companies, play an exhibition match.
